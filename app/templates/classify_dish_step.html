Main Objective

From the collection of tray images, we need to classify which station / restaurant it comes from.

The classification will be helpful for pairing the trays, and doing data analysis related to each station.

Dish Type

There are 6 main stations in the LG1 canteen, namely, bbq, delicacies, two choices, veggies, teppanyaki and japanese. However, we will ignore veggies since it has a few customers.
